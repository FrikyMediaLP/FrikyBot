FrikyBot Data and Cookie Usage

There are 3 Types of "Cookies": LocalStorage, SessionStorage and Cookies.

All "Cookies" are stored ON YOUR DEVICE BUT only Cookies are send / accessable to the server.

"Cookies" are used to provide Authentication over all pages (so you only need to log in ONCE), they provide a more plesant experience browsing, by storing frequently fetched data and much more...

Below are ALL "Cookies" the Bot uses:

LocalStorage - Persistent Storage

NO DATA FETCHED

SessionStorage - Data is deleted when Tab/Session is closed.

NO DATA FETCHED

Cookies - Data sent to/by the server.

NO DATA FETCHED
